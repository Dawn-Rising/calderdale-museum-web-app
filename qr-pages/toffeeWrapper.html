Toffee Wrapper








The Toffee Wrapper was a machine used to wrap toffee instead of people wrapping the sweets by hand, and would do so at a rate of almost 600 toffees a minute. This machine was made by Mackintosh (Not to be confused with Macintosh computers - Or now known as Apple) and are now known as Nestlé. The Nestlé factory is still in Halifax and still makes quality street like this machine wraps, but uses much newer technology to do so.
